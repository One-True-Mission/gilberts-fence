Our Story

A family company built on hard work, honest prices, and fences that stand straight for years.

2011 In the Trade Since
From Subcontractor to Family Company

Built With Family Support

In 2011, Gilbert started working as a fence subcontractor for a major retail chain in Austin, TX. He spent more than three years on those crews, learning everything the trade could teach him and gaining as much experience as possible.

He wanted to build something for himself, but most of all for his family. So in 2014, with his family behind him, Gilbert's Fence was born. Family owned and operated from day one, the company has grown on three simple commitments: great customer service, quality fence installations, and the best prices in town.

Today we serve homeowners, businesses, and landowners across Taylor, Austin, and the surrounding areas. Expect only the best service from Gilbert's Fence. Our reputation is your guarantee.
