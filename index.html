The Fence Crafters

A Family Company Built One Fence at a Time

Gilbert started installing fences in Austin back in 2011. In 2014, with his family behind him, he launched Gilbert's Fence. Ten years later the mission has not changed: great customer service, quality installations, and the best prices in town.

Every fence we build starts with the ground it stands on. Posts go 30 inches deep with generous concrete, so your fence stays straight through Texas heat, wind, and storms.

  • Free on-site estimates, same day or next day
  • Quality pickets and metal posts on every build
  • Full cleanup when the job is done
